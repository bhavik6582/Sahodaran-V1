UNAIDS Civil Society Award (2006)

Recognized for outstanding commitment to HIV/AIDS prevention and support at the national level.

Awarded by the Family Planning Association of India for impactful work with marginalized communities.

Honored for extraordinary contribution to the Red Ribbon Express HIV/AIDS awareness initiative.

First MSM CBO in South India
